The following UNIX headers are required:

netinet/in.h: ntohl, htonl, ntohs, htons (unless ABCE_NO_NETINET)
libgen.h: dirname for amyplanyydirparse (if DIRPARSE defined)
errno.h: standard errno constants in POSIX
sys/mman.h: mmap, munmap, potentially madvise (unless ABCE_NO_MMAP)
unistd.h: sysconf of _SC_PAGE_SIZE (unless ABCE_NO_MMAP)

Also, GCC's __builtin_expect is required.

Some test programs and amyplanyydomemparse require fmemopen in stdio.h.
