The following UNIX headers are required:

netinet/in.h: ntohl, htonl, ntohs, htons
libgen.h: dirname for amyplanyydirparse
errno.h: standard errno constants in POSIX
string.h: strdup
sys/mman.h: mmap, munmap, potentially madvise
unistd.h: sysconf of _SC_PAGE_SIZE

Also, GCC's __builtin_expect is required.

Some test programs and amyplanyydomemparse require fmemopen in stdio.h.
